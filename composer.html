Biography

Kai-Young Chan is an Associate Professor of music composition and an academic scholar whose work sits at the intersection of linguistic nuance and contemporary art music.

Recipient of the CASH Golden Sail Music Award, his works are performed globally by the Pittsburgh Symphony and Hong Kong Philharmonic, and published by Universal Edition.
